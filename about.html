The members of Indecent Degenerates have been jamming together for nearly 18 years. The dark-wave rock duo met in middle school and learned to play music together. Made up of Britt Caudle and Logan Meadows, Indecent Degenerates is here to shake up the music scene and offer people something new, but reminicent of the past.

Whether you listen to music to dance to the beat or you are looking for fresh guitar solos and funky bass lines, Indecent Degenerates is for you.
